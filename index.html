Welcome to the U.S. Army on Habbo Hotel: Origins!

Established on July 17, 2024, the U.S. Military: Army (USM) is a classic 2005-like army on Habbo Hotel: Origins. We promote personnel based on time served and effort. We do not offer pay. We do not sell ranks.

Join our Discord

Why Join Us?

  • Friendly and fun community
  • Daily to weekly rare furni giveaways to random personnel ranked Specialist and above who are active in our HQ
  • Private social events and games with prizes
  • Rise up the ranks as a personal accomplishment
  • It's entirely free: all of our tasks and activities allow you to have fun on Habbo without needing to purchase Credits

Uniforms are not required* on Fridays (based on US Eastern Time)

*Recruits must wear a uniform still on Fridays, until they complete Basic Training.
